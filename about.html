Defying the Stale Conventions of the Past.

WHAT'S BEHIND is a media platform that specializes in content creation and curating platforms, with the goal of cultivating arts behind modern culture; with the partner's prowess about the local scene. It recognizes the artists' lack of medium and media exposure.

WHAT'S BEHIND is able to produce original content that highlights the creativity of an artist; an in-depth look into their craft by the creators themselves. It builds connections and community for fans to interact and discover more about the artist. The lack of a platform surrounding this matter was brought to light by WHAT'S BEHIND by providing a cultural hub for artists to empower themselves, through their means.
